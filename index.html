BrainLove: A Culture of Curiosity

BrainLove is a 501(c)3 Non-profit dedicated to proliferating science and a culture of curiosity. Bringing together communities, members of distinguished institutions and hard-working people, BrainLove shares scientific art and knowledge in an open and welcoming setting.The BrainLove team works tirelessly to deliver new and engaging installations and events year round.
